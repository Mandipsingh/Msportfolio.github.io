What is a Glucometer?

A glucometer is blood sugar measurement device which gives instant readings even when you are on the go. It helps you track the amount of glucose in your blood at various intervals during the day according to your meal and exercise patterns.

How does a glucometer help a diabetic?

A compact device to help diabetics take regular readings during the day, store them for quick reference and track blood sugar patterns to analyse the medication and treatment
